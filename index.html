How to Cook Cavatappi

By Larry Linguini

  1. Bring a pot of water to a roaring boil
    • NOTE: Make sure this is a true boil, check the temperature so it is exactly 212°F (100°C)
  2. Pour in your box of Cavatappi noodles
  3. Let the noodles heat up for exactly one minute
  4. Add 3.27g of salt
  5. Let the noodles cook for an additional 8 minutes and 37 seconds
  6. Quickly remove them from the heat and strain them
  7. Enjoy your perfect Cavatappi!
